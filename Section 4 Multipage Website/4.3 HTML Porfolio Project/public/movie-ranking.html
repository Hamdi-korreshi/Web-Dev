Good Movies

Always in the Mood for These

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Spider-man is all about learning how and when to use your powers responsible, way better than the new one.

Dope

Living in a rough area and trying to escape while being really social awkward spoke to my teenage self. Good character development, funny and a great watch.

Wall-E

When I was young the strange wonder of seeing a trash compacting robot fall in love while traveling the cosmos and saving Earth, was mesmerizing. My mother said it was the only movie that I sat down to fully watch as a child. I thought that would fade with time yet when I went back to watch it, my butt was glues the same way.
